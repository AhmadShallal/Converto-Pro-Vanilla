Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on our website

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners. Cookies enhance user experience by remembering your preferences and enabling certain website features.

2. How We Use Cookies

Converto uses cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies remember your choices and settings to enhance your experience (such as language preferences and display settings).
  • Analytics Cookies: We use these to understand how visitors interact with our website. They help us analyze and improve our services.
  • Marketing Cookies: These track your online activity to help advertisers deliver more relevant advertising or to limit the number of times you see an ad.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Session Cookies

These temporary cookies are erased when you close your browser. They enable the website to remember what you've done during your visit.

3.2 Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device until they expire or you delete them. They help us recognize you as a returning visitor and adapt our website to your preferences.

3.3 First-Party Cookies

These are cookies placed by our website domain directly.

3.4 Third-Party Cookies

These are cookies placed by other domains. We use third-party services such as Google Analytics, which place cookies to help us understand how visitors use our site.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences. You can:

  • Delete cookies from your device
  • Block cookies by activating the setting on your browser that allows you to refuse all or some cookies
  • Set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie

Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, certain features of our website may not function correctly.

Browser-specific instructions:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to Our Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are using cookies.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@convertpro.com.
