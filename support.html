Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I install Converto?

After purchase, you'll receive download access to all template files. Simply upload the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files to your web server. For detailed installation instructions, please refer to our documentation.

Can I use Converto on multiple websites?

The number of websites you can use Converto on depends on your license type. The Standard license allows use on a single website, Premium on up to 5 websites, and Extended offers unlimited usage. Check our pricing page for more details.

Do you offer custom modifications?

Yes, we offer custom modification services for Converto. Please contact us with details about your requirements, and we'll provide a quote for the work.

How often do you release updates?

We typically release minor updates monthly and major feature updates quarterly. All updates are included with your purchase for the duration of your support period. Check our updates page for the latest releases.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if Converto doesn't meet your needs. Please contact our support team to process your refund request.
