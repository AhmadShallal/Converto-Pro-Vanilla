Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing plans

What is included in the Single Site License?

A Single Site License allows you to use ConvertPro on one domain or project. It includes 6 months of support and all future updates released during your support period.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. You'll only pay the difference between your current plan and the new plan you're upgrading to.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with ConvertPro within 14 days of purchase, contact our support team for a full refund.

What happens when my support period ends?

You can continue using ConvertPro after your support period ends, but you won't receive updates or support. You can renew your support at a discounted rate.

Can I use ConvertPro for client projects?

The Extended license allows you to use ConvertPro for client projects with no limitations. The Premium license allows usage on up to 5 sites, which can include client projects.
